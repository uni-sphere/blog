unisphere Virtual Learning Environment

With unisphere you can

Manage your classes.

Share your lessons.

Submit your assignments.

Unisphere's key fonctionnality is to enable seamless file sharing between students and teachers. Teachers can upload their entire syllabus with a single drag and drop and students can hand-in their assignments in seconds.
